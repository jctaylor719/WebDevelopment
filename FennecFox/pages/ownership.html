Fennec Fox Ownership

The fennec fox is considered an exotic pet. They can cost anywhere between $2,500 and $3,500, which does not include the price of shipping (source). It is legal to own a fennec fox everywhere in the United States except:

  • Missouri
  • Minnesota
  • Nevada
  • Washington

It is not recommended to have fennec foxes around young children because they bite, and the fennec fox's bones are very fragile. Besides that, fennecs are very fun to have around! There are also many reasons to own a fennec, mainly being:

  1. How cute they are!
  2. How nice they are!
  3. How sweet they are!
  4. Did I mention how cute they are?

Try and find the region fennecs can be found!
